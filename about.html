My journey
2019
Undergrad 🎓
During my pre-final year at undergrad, I was introduced briefly to the concepts of graphics and UX in one of the workshops. The topic instantly grabbed my interest, and the next moment, I found myself searching the Internet about everything related to this field.
Early 2021 - Mid 2023
Development experience 💼
Driven by a passion for technology, I actively sought collaborations with global companies. During the internship at Cognizant, I delved into front-end development and SharePoint, gaining hands-on experience. As a full-time employee at Infosys, I played a key role in Java automation and server management, enhancing critical system functionality for 2 years.
Early 2022
Exploring 🤝
After doing a bunch of courses and certifications, I recognized my love for design. I connected with many people in the field of design, and came to know what exactly this is about. People with a variety of expertise, such as User Research, UX Design, Product Design, UX Writing, Graphic Design, Visual Design etc. helped me take the right steps.
Late 2022
Getting ready 👨‍💻
I got my hands dirty with design by taking up some passion projects on my own and started uploading my work on Dribbble to get feedback from experts in the field. I realized the need to expand my knowledge and skillset to grow more.
Early 2023
The design journey begins 📚
Based on the recommendations of experienced and skilled professionals in the UX community, I took a proactive step towards advancing my career by enrolling in a specialized professional bootcamp at DesignBoat UI UX School. I started uploading my projects on Behance.
Mid 2023
UI UX internship 🎉
I got a real-world design experience by interning at a service-based startup, Pixerea Solutions. I learned gathering client requirements and designing websites and native mobile applications tailored to meet client's specific goals and expectations
Late 2023
Full-time job 🚀
I joined Vobble, an early stage product-based startup, as a full-time UI UX Designer. Being the only designer in the company came as an exiciting challenge with a lot of responsibilities and a chance to make a significant impact on the company's product design.
What next?
I'm excited and hope to attend graduate program starting Fall 2024! 🤩
